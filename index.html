About the artist:

Denver-based Chroma Lips make music built from layered guitars and synth, with dual male & female vocals over a driving rhythm section. The group takes a DIY approach, writing, recording, and creating artwork from their hand-built home studio. Motivated by a passion for travel and a refusal to settle into the predictable, with the release of chromaZone, have laid the groundwork for a project built to stay in motion.

Chroma Lips debut album: "chromaZone"

Transmitting from a universe of texture and motion, a sonic atmosphere of guitars and synthesizers blur into one as the record brings you into a steady, motorik-driven momentum.

Self-produced and recorded, fronting members Harrison Edwards and Violet De Vries fashioned the record in their hand-built DIY studio.

Across ten tracks, chromaZone moves through desert highways, derelict cityscapes, electric rock clubs, and late-night reflection.

Available everywhere June 22, 2026
